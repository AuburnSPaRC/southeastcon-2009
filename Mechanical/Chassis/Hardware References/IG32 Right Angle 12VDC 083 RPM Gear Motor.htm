These gear motors are made with steel gears (not plastic or sintered metal). They run great and we've tested them extensively. These motors have right angle gear boxes on them. We sampled many other manufactures and the gears would strip or the windings would burn up, we had no failures with these.

We recommend controlling the motors with one of our motor speed controllers. See our motor controller support page for more information. We also recommend you use our wiring kit to wire your motors to control the electronic noise generated from any DC motor. See our motor wiring support page for more information.

These motors have a rated stall torque of  24 kgf-cm! They utilize a 1:71 gear reduction. The gear motor is 12VDC.
