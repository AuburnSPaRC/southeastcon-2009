These hubs are designed to provide a simple, light weight, low profile mounting solution for our wheels with a 1/2" Hex Mount. They are CNC machined Aluminum and feature a durable black oxide finish. The hubs are supplied with mounting hardware as pictured.

To mount a wheel with these hubs is a simple three step process:

Step 1: Slide the hub onto the shaft and lock it in place with the supplied set screw.
NOTE: You will need 1/16" hex wrench to tighten the set screw.
Step 2: Slide the wheel onto the hub.
Step 3: Place the supplied snap ring in the groove to hold the wheel in place.
NOTE: You will need a pair of external snap ring pliers for this step.


Hub Specifications
Mounting : 1/2in Hex
For Shaft : 6mm
Weight* : 0.24oz (6.8g)
Set Screw : 6-32 x 1/8in
Num Set Screws : 2
Snap Ring : 1/2in External
Material : Aluminum
*Weight includes hardware

You can find replacement snap rings and set screws as well as machine keys and related tools in Wheel & Hub Accessories
